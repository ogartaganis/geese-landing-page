Storyboard: Two Geese in Colombia

Travel Log - Expanded Expedition

This storyboard chronicles the adventures of two traveling geese as they explore the vibrant landscapes and cities of Colombia.

Scene 1: Daytime at Monserrate, Bogotá

High above the capital, the geese explore the Monserrate overlook under the bright Colombian sun. One of them is already feeling the heat and slowly turning red from sunburn!

Scene 2: Winery with a New Friend in Villa de Leyva

The duo finds tranquility among the grapevines and white-washed colonial architecture of a winery in Villa de Leyva. They are joined by a lovely and friendly labrador.

Scene 3: Arrival in Cartagena

The journey continues in the colorful, historic streets of Cartagena's Old City.

Scene 4: Friends at Aviario Nacional

In the lush Aviario Nacional, the geese make new friends among bright pink flamingos and colorful parrots. One of them even tries to master the flamingo's one-legged stance!

Scene 5: Relaxing at Casa en el Agua

The geese head to the San Bernardo Archipelago to visit the famous floating hostel, "Casa en el Agua", for some well-deserved relaxation.

Scene 6: Rooftop Hot Tub in Medellín

The travelers arrive in the City of Eternal Spring. To cap off the night, they relax in a luxurious rooftop hot tub, taking in the glowing city lights of Medellín below.
